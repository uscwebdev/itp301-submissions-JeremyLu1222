Project Target Audience

I want to mimic an private jet booking website since I recently found lots advertisement introducing this business. And lots of them have reaaly good web design, so I want to tried to do it by myself.

Intended Audience

Potential customers who may afford booking private jet tickets and anyone who is interested in this field

Design and Style

Website That Insipired me

  • Aero

The main color I decided to use is black since I believe that black will make it Sense of luxury

General Design

Like Aero, I would like to use black as main color to show sense of luxury. However, I want to combine hyer and aero which create a sense of a luxury thing flying in the sky

Sections

I am planning to have four sections. They are Picture Gallery of the fleet, Popular destination, The service and a booking link.
